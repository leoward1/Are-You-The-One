1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, age, and email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photos and video introductions
  • Location data (for safety check-ins only)

Verification Data:

  • Selfie verification data
  • Optional identity verification

Usage Data:

  • App activity, matches, and interactions
  • Device information and app logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide matching and compatibility services
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • Send updates, safety alerts, and notifications

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Verification and identity service providers
  • Payment processors (Apple App Store)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard protections including:

  • End-to-end encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud storage via Supabase with row-level security
  • Access controls and rate limiting

No system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password.

5. User Control & Your Rights

  • Edit or delete your profile at any time
  • Request full account and data deletion from within the app (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Control location permissions in your device settings

6. Location Data

Location is used only for Safety Check-ins when you choose to start one. We do not track your location in the background outside of an active safety session.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Daily.co — voice and video calls
  • Apple App Store — in-app purchases

8. Children's Privacy

This app is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
