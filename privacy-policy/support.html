Support Center

Need help with Are You The One? We're here for you. Browse the topics below or reach out directly.

Account Issues

  • Can't log in: Try resetting your password via the login screen. Check your spam folder for the reset email.
  • Forgot email: Contact us with your name and we'll look up your account.
  • Delete account: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

Matches & Messages

  • No matches showing: Make sure your profile is fully completed and photos are uploaded.
  • Can't send messages: You can only message mutual matches. Some features unlock progressively.
  • Unread badge not clearing: Open the chat and wait a moment for messages to mark as read.

Calls

  • Call not connecting: Ensure both users have a stable internet connection. Voice/video calls require a mutual match with unlocked call stage.
  • No sound during call: Check that your device is not on silent and microphone permission is granted in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions & Purchases

  • Cancel subscription: Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Are You The One → Cancel.
  • Restore purchases: Go to Profile → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • Billing issues: Contact Apple Support at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Safety

  • Block a user: Open the chat → tap the flag icon → Block User.
  • Report a user: Open the chat → tap the flag icon → Report Profile.
  • SOS / Emergency: Use the Safety tab to set up check-ins and emergency contacts.
