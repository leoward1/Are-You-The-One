Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

Welcome to Are You The One ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender, city, and profile photos you provide during registration.
  • Profile Content: Bio, interests, height, occupation, lifestyle tags, and any other information you add to your profile.
  • Messages: Chat messages, date suggestions, and in-app game data exchanged with matches.
  • Photos & Videos: Images and video introductions you upload to your profile.
  • Location Data: Approximate location used for safety check-ins and distance-based discovery (only when you grant permission).
  • Device Information: Push notification token, device type, and OS version for notification delivery.
  • Usage Data: Swipe actions, match interactions, and feature usage for app improvement.
  • Payment Information: Subscription and in-app purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To match you with compatible users based on preferences and gender.
  • To deliver real-time chat, voice, and video call features.
  • To send push notifications for matches, messages, and safety alerts.
  • To enforce safety features including check-ins and SOS functionality.
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms.
  • To improve and personalise the app experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile information is visible to potential matches.
  • Service Providers: Supabase (database & auth), Daily.co (video/voice calls), Expo (push notifications), and Apple (payments). These providers are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect safety.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app settings, which permanently removes your profile, photos, messages, and all associated data from our systems.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security in our database, and input sanitisation. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Are You The One is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.
  • Data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for new matches, messages, and safety alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app's notification preferences.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase – Database and authentication
  • Daily.co – Voice and video calls
  • Expo – Push notifications
  • Apple – App distribution and payments
