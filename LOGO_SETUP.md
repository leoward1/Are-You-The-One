### 5. Notification Icon (96x96px, white icon on transparent background)

## Quick Setup

1. **Save your logo** as `icon.png` in the `src\assets` folder
2. **Copy it** to create the other required files:
   - Copy `icon.png` → `splash.png`
   - Copy `icon.png` → `adaptive-icon.png`
   - Copy `icon.png` → `favicon.png`
   - Copy `icon.png` → `notification-icon.png`

## Color Scheme

I've updated the app colors to match your logo:
- **Background Color**: `#8B1538` (Deep burgundy red from your logo)

## After Adding Files

Once you've saved the images, run:
```powershell
npx expo start
```

The app will now display your beautiful logo! 🎉
